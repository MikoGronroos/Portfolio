Paint Drifters

Details

Role(s)

  • Lead gameplay programmer
  • Gameplay designer

Battle your friends in this addictive local multiplayer racing game.

Drive fast and make circles. Fill the circles with paint and claim more land to be victorious. Be prepared you must fill your paint bucket in the main puddle of paint.

Collect special abilities like fill paint bucket and speed boost.

When the timer runs out the player who has claimed the most land wins.
